## System Settings

### Launch at Login

Control whether Ella starts automatically when you log into your Mac.

| Setting | Behavior |
|---------|----------|
| Enabled | Ella starts automatically at login |
| Disabled | You must manually open Ella |

<Tip>
  Enable Launch at Login if you use Ella frequently. This ensures it's always ready when you need it.
</Tip>

### Show App in Dock

Control whether Ella appears in your Mac's Dock when the main window is closed.

| Setting | Behavior |
|---------|----------|
| Enabled | Ella icon remains visible in Dock |
| Disabled | Ella runs only in the menu bar |

<Note>
  Even with this disabled, Ella remains accessible from the menu bar icon in the top-right of your screen.
</Note>

## Notifications

Configure how Ella notifies you about dictation activity.

### Transcription Sound

Play an audio sound when transcription finishes processing.

| Setting | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Enabled | Plays a sound when transcription is complete |
| Disabled | No sound plays |

This provides audio confirmation that your dictation has been processed and inserted.

### Transcription Notification

Show a macOS notification when transcription completes.

| Setting | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Enabled | Shows system notification with transcribed text |
| Disabled | No notification shown |

<Note>
  Useful when you want visual confirmation of what was transcribed, especially for longer dictations.
</Note>

### Recording Status

Notify when recording starts and stops.

| Setting | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Enabled | Shows notifications for recording state changes |
| Disabled | No recording status notifications |

This helps you know when Ella is actively listening, which can be useful in noisy environments.
