## Profile Information

### Full Name

You can update your display name in Ella:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the Ella menu bar icon and select **Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Account">
    Navigate to the **Account** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Name">
    Edit the **Full Name** field with your first and last name.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to update your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your name must include both a first and last name to save successfully.
</Note>

### Email Address

Your email address is displayed in Account settings but cannot be changed directly. If you need to change your email, please contact support.

## Sign-In Method

Ella supports the following sign-in methods:

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Google | Sign in with your Google account |
| Email & Password | Traditional email/password login |

### For Google Users

If you signed in with Google, your sign-in method is displayed in Account settings. To sign in, simply use the same Google account.

### For Password Users

If you created an account with email and password, you can change your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Go to Settings, then the Account tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request Password Reset">
    Click **Change Password**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    You'll receive a password reset email at your registered address.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Follow the link in the email to set a new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign Out

To sign out of your Ella account:

1. Click on your name in the side navigation
2. Select **Sign Out** from the flyout menu
3. You'll be logged out and returned to the sign-in screen

<Note>
  You'll need to sign in again to use Ella after signing out.
</Note>

## Delete Account

<Warning>
  **Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.** All data associated with your account will be removed, including your transcription history and settings.
</Warning>

### What Happens When You Delete Your Account

- All your transcriptions are permanently deleted
- Your user profile and settings are removed
- If you have a Pro subscription, it will be cancelled
- You will not receive a refund for remaining subscription time

### Delete Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Go to Settings, then the Account tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Delete Account">
    Scroll down and click **Delete Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Type "delete" in the confirmation field to confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Deletion">
    Click **Delete Account** to permanently delete your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Pro Subscribers

If you have an active Pro subscription:

| Billing Type | What Happens |
|--------------|--------------|
| Annual (with time remaining) | No refund for unused time |
| Monthly | Subscription ends immediately |

<Note>
  Consider downgrading to Basic before deleting your account if you want to preserve your subscription until the end of your billing period.
</Note>
