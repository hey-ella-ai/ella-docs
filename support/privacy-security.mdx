## How Ella Handles Your Data

### Voice Audio

| What Happens | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| Captured | Only while you're actively dictating |
| Transmitted | Encrypted, sent to transcription service |
| Processed | Converted to text |
| **Stored** | **Never** — audio is discarded immediately |

<Info>
  We do not record, store, or retain your voice data. Audio exists only during the brief moment of transcription processing.
</Info>

### Transcribed Text

| What Happens | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| Processed | On secure servers |
| Returned | To your device for insertion |
| Server Storage | **None** — text is not retained |
| Local Storage | Optional history on your device only |

### Transcription History

Your transcription history is stored **locally on your Mac**:

- Location: `~/Library/Application Support/com.ella.desktop/`
- Encryption: Protected by your Mac's security
- Access: Only you can access it
- Cloud sync: **None** — stays on your device

<Note>
  If you uninstall Ella and delete this folder, all local history is permanently removed.
</Note>

## Data We Collect

### Account Information

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Email address | Account identification, communication | Encrypted on servers |
| Name (optional) | Personalization | Encrypted on servers |
| Payment info | Subscription processing | Handled by payment provider Stripe (never touches our servers) |

### Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to improve Ella:

| Collected | Not Collected |
|-----------|---------------|
| Feature usage patterns | Transcription content |
| Error reports | Voice recordings |
| App performance metrics | Personal identifiers |
| Aggregate statistics | Browsing history |

<Accordion title="What does anonymous analytics include?">
  - Which features are used (e.g., "hotkey activated")
  - Session duration
  - Error types (not content)
  - Device type and OS version
  - Geographic region (country level)

  This data cannot identify you or reveal your transcription content.
</Accordion>

### Error Reporting

When Ella crashes or encounters errors:

| Sent | Not Sent |
|------|----------|
| Stack trace | Transcription text |
| App version | Audio data |
| OS version | Personal files |
| Device type | Account details |

## Security Measures

### Data Transmission

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.3
- **Certificate pinning**: Prevents man-in-the-middle attacks
- **Secure endpoints**: Industry-standard cloud infrastructure

### Authentication

- **Enterprise-grade authentication**: Industry-leading authentication provider
- **PKCE flow**: Secure OAuth implementation
- **Keychain storage**: Credentials stored in macOS Keychain
- **Session management**: Secure token handling

### Infrastructure

- **Cloud providers**: SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
- **Data centers**: Geographically distributed, physically secured
- **Access controls**: Strict internal access policies
- **Regular audits**: Security assessments and penetration testing

## Third-Party Services

Ella partners with trusted third-party services to provide core functionality. All partners have been vetted for privacy compliance and security standards.

| Purpose | Data Shared | How It's Protected |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Transcription & Formatting | Audio & Text (not stored) | Encrypted, immediately discarded |
| Authentication | Email, password | Industry-standard encryption |
| Payment processing | Payment details | PCI-compliant, never touches our servers |
| Analytics | Anonymous usage | No personal identifiers |
| Error tracking | Crash reports | No transcription content |

We carefully select partners who share our commitment to privacy. Each service uses data only for its stated purpose and is contractually bound to protect your information.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can Ella employees see my transcriptions?">
    No. Transcription content is never stored on our servers. Your local history is only on your device. We have no technical ability to access your transcriptions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my voice used to train AI models?">
    No. Your voice data is never used for training. It's processed for transcription and immediately discarded.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I delete my account?">
    Your account and all associated data on our servers and local data are permanently deleted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Ella HIPAA compliant?">
    For healthcare use cases requiring HIPAA compliance, please contact us to discuss your specific requirements and our Business Associate Agreement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Ella share data with advertisers?">
    No. We never sell or share your data with advertisers. We have no advertising business model.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long is my account data retained?">
    Account data is retained while your account is active. After deletion, data is removed immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
