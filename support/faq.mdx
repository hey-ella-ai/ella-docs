## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Ella?">
    Ella is a voice-to-text application for macOS that lets you dictate text in any application. Simply speak naturally, and Ella converts your speech to text and inserts it at your cursor position.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Ella work?">
    1. You activate Ella with a hotkey (default: `Fn` for Push to Talk)
    2. Ella captures your voice through your microphone
    3. Your speech is sent securely to our transcription service
    4. The transcribed text is inserted where your cursor is positioned

    The entire process takes just seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Ella free?">
    Ella offers a free Basic plan with 3,000 words per week. For unlimited dictation, you can upgrade to Pro for $8/month or $80/year. See [Plans & Pricing](/settings/plans-pricing) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What platforms does Ella support?">
    Ella currently supports **macOS** (both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs). Windows support is planned for the future.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What macOS version do I need?">
    Ella requires macOS 13.1 (Ventura) or later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features & Usage

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Ella work in every application?">
    Yes! Ella works in any application where you can type text — browsers, email clients, code editors, messaging apps, documents, notes, and more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Ella offline?">
    Ella requires an internet connection for transcription. However, there's a brief offline grace period that allows limited dictation when connectivity is temporarily lost.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages does Ella support?">
    Ella currently supports English. Additional language support is coming soon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add punctuation?">
    Our smart formatter will automatically add punctuation based on context. 
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I customize the activation hotkey?">
    Yes! Go to Settings → General to change your hotkey. You can use single keys (like Caps Lock) or key combinations. See [Dictation](/features/dictation).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between dictation modes?">
    - **Push to Talk**: Hold key to record, release to stop (default: `Fn`)
    - **Hands-Free Mode**: Press once to start, press again to stop (default: `Ctrl + Space`)
    - **Menu Bar**: Click "Start Recording" from the Ella menu bar icon

    See [Dictation](/features/dictation) for detailed comparison.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add custom words to Ella?">
    Yes! Use the custom dictionary to add vocabulary words and replacement rules. Go to Settings → Dictionary → Add New. See [Dictionary](/features/dictionary).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my voice data stored?">
    No. Audio is processed for transcription and immediately discarded. We don't store recordings of your voice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my transcribed text stored?">
    Transcription content is processed and returned to you but not stored on our servers. Your local transcription history is stored only on your device.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can access my transcriptions?">
    Only you. Transcription history is stored locally on your Mac, not in the cloud. No one at Ella can see your transcriptions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the transmission secure?">
    Yes. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We follow industry best practices for security.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What data does Ella collect?">
    We collect minimal analytics data (usage patterns, error reports) to improve the product. This never includes transcription content. See [Privacy & Security](/support/privacy-security) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
    When you first launch Ella, you'll be prompted to sign up. You can create an account using:
    - **Google** — Sign in with your Google account
    - **Email & Password** — Create an account with your email address
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and Apple Pay through Stripe.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade to Pro?">
    Go to Settings → Plans & Billing → Upgrade to Pro. Choose monthly or annual billing and complete payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Yes. We offer full refunds within 14 days of purchase. Contact support@heyella.co with your account email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Go to Settings → Plans & Billing → Manage Subscription → Cancel. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I reach my word limit?">
    On the Basic plan, when you use 3,000 words in a week, dictation pauses until Sunday at midnight when it resets. You can upgrade to Pro for immediate unlimited access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused words roll over?">
    No. The word limit resets each week regardless of usage. Unused words don't carry over.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why does Ella need Accessibility permission?">
    Accessibility permission allows Ella to insert text at your cursor position in any application. Without it, Ella can't type text for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does Ella need Microphone permission?">
    Microphone permission is required to capture your voice for transcription. Without it, Ella can't hear what you say.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Ella work with external microphones?">
    Yes! You can select your preferred microphone in Settings → General. Ella supports USB mics, headsets, Bluetooth mics, and has an Auto-detect option that uses your system default.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much internet bandwidth does Ella use?">
    Ella uses minimal bandwidth. Audio is compressed before transmission. A typical 30-second dictation uses less than 100KB.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will Ella slow down my Mac?">
    No. Ella is designed to be lightweight. It uses minimal CPU and memory, even during active transcription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ella isn't transcribing my speech">
    1. Check microphone permission in System Settings
    2. Verify your mic is selected and working
    3. Ensure you're holding the hotkey (Push-to-Talk mode)
    4. Restart Ella

    See [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) for more solutions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Text isn't appearing where I want it">
    1. Verify accessibility permission in System Settings
    2. Make sure cursor is in a text field before dictating
    3. Don't move cursor during transcription

    See [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) for more help.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My hotkey isn't working">
    1. Check accessibility permission
    2. Verify the hotkey in Settings
    3. Check for conflicts with other apps
    4. Try a different hotkey

    See [Dictation](/features/dictation) for configuration help.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
