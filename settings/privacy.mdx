Ella is designed with privacy in mind. Your voice data is processed securely and your transcriptions are stored locally on your device.

## Data Storage

All your transcription data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your Mac. Your transcriptions are never uploaded to external servers unless you explicitly share them.

| Data Type | Storage Location | Retention |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Transcriptions | Local SQLite database | Until you delete them |
| Settings | Local preferences file | Persistent |
| Usage statistics | Local + cloud sync | For quota tracking |

## Delete All Transcriptions

You can permanently delete all locally stored transcriptions at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the Ella menu bar icon and select **Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Privacy">
    Navigate to the **Privacy** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete Transcriptions">
    Click **Delete All Transcripts**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Review the confirmation dialog showing the number of transcripts that will be deleted, then confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **This action is permanent and cannot be undone.** All your locally stored transcriptions will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

## Voice Data Handling

Ella processes your voice data securely:

- **During dictation**: Audio is captured and sent to our transcription service for processing
- **After transcription**: Audio is immediately discarded â€” we do not store recordings
- **Transcribed text**: Stored locally on your device only

<Note>
  Your voice recordings are never stored on our servers. Only the resulting text is saved locally on your Mac.
</Note>

## Analytics & Error Reporting

Ella collects minimal analytics to improve the app experience:

| Data Collected | Purpose | Contains Personal Data |
|----------------|---------|----------------------|
| App usage patterns | Improve features | No |
| Error reports | Fix bugs | No |
| Feature usage | Prioritize development | No |

<Note>
  Analytics never includes your transcription content. Only aggregate usage patterns and error information are collected to help improve Ella.
</Note>
